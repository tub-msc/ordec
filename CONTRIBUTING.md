# Contributing to ORDeC

ORDeC is in an early development phase. We are building in the open, but much of the internal architecture is not yet fixed and may change significantly.

**If you are interested in extending ORDeC in a particular direction or adding a feature, please reach out to us before spending time writing code.** You can do this by:

- Opening a GitHub issue to discuss your idea
- Sending us an email

Pull requests that add or change features without prior discussion should expect to be rejected. This is not meant to discourage contributions -- we simply want to avoid wasted effort on work that may conflict with ongoing architectural changes or planned directions.

**Small bugfixes are welcome at any time** and do not require prior discussion.

As ORDeC matures and the architecture stabilizes, we hope to transition to a more open and collaborative development model.
